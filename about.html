Hi! My name is Colleen Duong and I am currently a 5th year student pursuing a Bachelors of Architecture degree at Carnegie Mellon University

I was born and raised in Honolulu, Hawaii. After deciding to travel away from my home island to the city of Pittsburgh to attend Carnegie Mellon University, I began to pursue a Bachelors of Architecture degree with a minor in Animation & Special Effects. I love to design and tell stories! In Architecture School we are encouraged to use our Imaginations to create something that we’ve never seen before. What I love about architecture is that we are utilizing our imaginations to just create an experience between people and architecture. I want to do something that tests my imagination to its limits.

I like to spend my free time doing community service, reading Young Adult Novels, and watching video game playthroughs on YouTube. When I’m not doing any of those or drawing and designing, I am probably staring at a chrome window with a million YouTube tutorial tabs trying to teach myself how to use a new program. Currently, I am trying to teach myself how to use Adobe AfterEffects to create a short animated clip or how to model on Blender.

If you have any book recommendations or want any book recommendations, feel free to message me! Wondering what I'm up to now?
