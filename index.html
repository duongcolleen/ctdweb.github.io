Hello! My name is Colleen.

I was born and raised in Honolulu, Hawaii. I'm currently pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Architecture and a Minor in Animation at Carnegie Mellon University. At school I enjoy designing, storytelling, modeling (maya, zbrush, rhinoceros), coding, working with machines (Robot Arm, Lasercutting, 3D Printing, etc.), and community service!

I love reading Young Adult novels during my free time and also watching movies, tv shows, and YouTube Gameplays. When I'm not reading or watching something, I like to try to teach myself new skills. Currently, I am trying to improve my newly learned skills with Maya and ZBrush to learn the basics of animation. Before I graduate from college I want to successfully be able to storyboard and create a short animation from scratch.
