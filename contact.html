Hi! My Name is Colleen Duong (COCO) and I am currently a Designer in San Francisco.

Born and raised in Hawai’i, I am constantly inspired by the world around me and am interested in designing a world that enhances the environment that we live in. I love to design and tell stories. In architecture we are encouraged to use our imagination to create something we’ve never seen before. It allows us to utilize our imaginations not in just creating buildings, but in designing an experience between people and the space they live within, digitally and physically. I want to do something that tests my imagination to its limits.

I like to spend my free time doing community service and reading. When I’m not doing either of those I am constantly drawing, sketching, or scrolling through social media to find inspiration. I also love to learn new skills! Currently I am teaching myself how to use Blender and After Effects.

Please feel free to message me if you want to chat or if you have any book recommendations!!
