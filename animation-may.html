CHARACTER SHEET

Meet May. She is a witch in training specializing in baking. In this world witches and other magical beings exist alongside humans. However, only some humans know about the existence of these magical beings. Students at this magic school learn to apply their skills to do more mundane things (like sciences, culinary, etc.). May likes to spend her entire day in front of the oven and is very clumsy and childlike. She is currently studying to get her witch's license so she can explore the world and learn new recipes. She always carries her trusty whisk/roller/sprinkle broom and her familiar is a one eyed cauldron-cooking pot.
