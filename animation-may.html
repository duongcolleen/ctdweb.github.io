May the Witch
She is a witch in training specializing in baking. She always carries her trusty whisk/roller/sprinkle broom and her familiar is a one eyed cauldron-cooking pot.

CHARACTER SHEET

Meet May. She is a witch in training specializing in baking. In this world witches and other magical beings exist alongside humans. However, only some humans know about the existence of these magical beings. Students at this magic school learn to apply their skills to do more mundane things (like sciences, culinary, etc.). May likes to spend her entire day in front of the oven and is very clumsy and childlike. She is currently studying to get her witch's license so she can explore the world and learn new recipes. She always carries her trusty whisk/roller/sprinkle broom and her familiar is a one eyed cauldron-cooking pot.

Skills: Design and Research


3D MODEL

May was originally modeled on ZBrush and then remeshed on Maya. Learning from my first attempt at modeling on maya and zbrush, I tried to model something new from scratch for this animation: a kitchen.

Skills: ZBrush Modeling, Maya Modeling and Remeshing, Substance Painter Texturing

STORYBOARD

Beginning shots show someone dancing (with their body movements) while cooking and then gradually revealing that there is something more to her than just being a baker. Then a final shot of her using her magic to bake.

Skills: ZBrush Modeling, Maya Modeling and Remeshing, Substance Painter Texturing
